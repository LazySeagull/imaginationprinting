ABOUT

About Me


Ben is a first year university student undertaking a full time double degree in both Mechatronic Engineering (Honours) and Computer Science. They has been playing with 3D printers for the past 4-5 years, and has been designing CAD/CAM models for even longer.

Ben has lead engineering clubs in high school, such as the international Rapidly Manufactured Rescue League team "Kings Legacy", who recently placed 3rd at Robocup, Sydney in 2019. Ben continues to provide their skills to the club, through the designing of new robots, to maintaining previous competitions robots.

Recently, they have been taking part of the international FIRST robotics competition, in which teams of high school students compete against each other using robots designed for specific tasks, such as shooting balls or climbing a beam.

They have been competing as part of the inaugural UWA FIRST robotics team, who intended to compete in the 2020 regional competition, however they were cut short due to the COVID-19 Virus epidemic. They hope to continue on as a mentor in future years.

Who we are.


Imagination Printing is a startup focused on making high quality 3D Printing and Modelling as affordable and widespread as possible. Inspired by the recent surge in technologies around the concept of 3D printing, Imagination Printing comes forward as the idea of a young student who felt driven to pursue the potential of 3D design and printing.

Now, we are armed with the latest industry technology to provide Australia with the highest quality prints, and years of expertise creating CAD/CAM models have made our team experts in what they do.

SERVICES

3D Printing


We offer high quality 3D printing services using our industry standard 3D printers. We offer a range of materials, specialising in PLA, in which we provide a wide range of colours, including white, silver and black.

We provide our customers with the abiity to adjust the desired quality of the print. Adjusting the quality of the print will change the total cost of the print job, allowing us at Imagination Printing to provide our customers with a extensive range of cost effective options.

Please contact us using our contact form (below) with any questions surrounding our 3D Printing service.

3D Modelling & Designing


Should you not be able to find the desired model you wish to print online, or you simply need something modelled with standary industry CAD/CAM tools, we offer the experience of several years of intensive use of programs such as AutoDesk Inventor, Fusion 360 and CATIA v5. These programs are used by companies such as Boeing Aerospace Company and Robotis Technologies.

First we will discuss what you need from the model, and all the features and requirements you wish to see from the model, and then our team of experts will assemble the model online.
We then go through a phase of where clients offer feedback, tweaking the model as they seem fit, with the assistance of our friendly staff. Once the model is completed we send you the model in a variety of formats available for 3D printing, showcasing and editing.
